Our Story

Where Every Fold
Tells a Story

Nestled on vibrant King Street in the heart of Newtown, Dumpling Room brings the soul of Pan-Asian cooking to Sydney's most colourful neighbourhood.

Our dumplings are crafted by hand each morning — thin, silky skins wrapped around perfectly seasoned fillings. From the umami-rich pork & chive to the delicate prawn & vegetable, every bite carries the warmth of a grandmother's kitchen and the precision of a seasoned chef.

Sit by the window, watch Newtown buzz outside, and let the flavours of Asia transport you — one dumpling at a time.
